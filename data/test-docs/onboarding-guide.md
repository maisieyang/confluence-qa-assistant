# New Hire Onboarding Guide

## Before Your First Day

Please complete the following before your start date:
1. Sign and return your employment contract to HR
2. Prepare your government-issued ID for verification
3. Download and install the company VPN client from https://vpn.company.com

## First Day Setup

### Account Access

New employees must apply for the following permissions within the first week:

1. **VPN Access** — Required for remote access to the corporate network. Apply via the IT Service Desk.
2. **GitLab Repository Access** — For accessing code repositories. Your team lead will grant access to relevant projects.
3. **JIRA Project Access** — For task tracking and project management. Request access through your manager.
4. **Confluence Space Access** — For documentation. Access is granted per-space by space administrators.
5. **Slack Workspace** — You will receive an automatic invitation to your company email.

### IT Service Desk

The IT Service Desk is available at https://it.company.com. Business hours are 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM SGT, Monday to Friday. Tickets are typically processed within 1–2 business days.

For urgent issues, contact the on-call engineer via Slack channel `#it-support`.

## Team Introduction

During your first week, your buddy will help you:
- Meet your team members and key stakeholders
- Understand the team's current projects and priorities
- Set up your development environment
- Review the team's coding standards and contribution guidelines

## Probation Period

The probation period is 3 months for all new hires. During this period:
- You will have bi-weekly 1:1s with your manager
- A mid-probation review at 6 weeks
- A final probation review at 12 weeks

## Company Policies

### Annual Leave
Full-time employees are entitled to **14 days** of annual leave per calendar year. Leave accrues monthly at 1.17 days per month. Unused leave can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days.

To apply for leave, submit a request through the HR portal at https://hr.company.com at least 3 business days in advance.

### Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to **14 days** of outpatient sick leave and **60 days** of hospitalization leave per year. A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days.

### Remote Work
The company follows a hybrid work model. Employees are expected to be in the office at least 3 days per week (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday). Remote work on Monday and Friday is permitted with manager approval.
