# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Eligible Expenses

### Business Travel
- **Flights**: Economy class for trips under 6 hours, business class for trips over 6 hours (requires VP approval)
- **Hotels**: Up to SGD 250/night in Singapore, up to USD 200/night for international travel
- **Ground transport**: Taxi/Grab for business purposes. Use the corporate Grab account when possible.
- **Meals during travel**: Up to SGD 80/day (no receipts needed under SGD 30)

### Client Entertainment
- **Client meals**: Up to SGD 150/person. Pre-approval required for groups > 5 people.
- **Client gifts**: Up to SGD 100/gift. No cash or cash equivalents.

### Professional Development
- **Conference tickets**: Up to SGD 2,000/event. Requires manager approval.
- **Online courses**: Up to SGD 500/year via the learning budget. Request through HR portal.
- **Books**: Up to SGD 200/year. No approval needed.

### Work Equipment
- **Peripherals** (keyboard, mouse, monitor): Up to SGD 500/year. No approval needed.
- **Ergonomic equipment**: Contact HR for office ergonomic assessment and equipment.

## How to Submit

1. Log in to the expense system at https://expenses.company.com
2. Click "New Expense Report"
3. Attach receipts (photos accepted)
4. Select the correct expense category and cost center
5. Submit for manager approval

### Deadlines
- Expenses must be submitted within **30 days** of the transaction
- Late submissions require Finance Manager approval
- Expenses older than 90 days will not be reimbursed

## Reimbursement Timeline

- Approved expenses are reimbursed in the next payroll cycle
- Payroll dates: 25th of each month
- For urgent reimbursements (> SGD 500), contact finance@company.com

## Corporate Credit Card

Senior employees (Manager level and above) may apply for a corporate credit card:
- Monthly limit: SGD 5,000
- Statements must be reconciled within 5 business days
- Personal expenses on corporate cards must be repaid immediately
- Apply through Finance team: finance@company.com
